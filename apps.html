Unnascan

Unnascan is a QR Scan & Pay app that integrates with M-PESA for instant mobile payments. Merchants and customers benefit from fast, secure transactions by scanning QR codes linked to till numbers, paybill numbers, or phone numbers.

  • Instant QR-based payments
  • Secure transaction logging
  • Merchant payout reconciliation
  • Cross-platform support (Android/iOS)

Unnapack

Unnapack is a smart school management and digital payment system. Schools can collect fees via M-PESA Paybill, send instant SMS receipts to parents, and manage records, exams, staff, transport, and assets — all in one platform.

  • Automated fee collection
  • Instant SMS & receipts
  • Exam, staff & transport tracking
  • Secure financial automation
