Unnascan

Unnascan is an innovative QR-based payment platform designed to simplify and accelerate mobile money transactions between businesses and customers. The system enables merchants to generate unique QR codes linked to their registered mobile numbers, allowing customers to make payments quickly and securely by simply scanning a QR code with their smartphone.

Currently, Unnascan supports phone number-based M-PESA payments, providing a seamless cashless payment experience for both merchants and customers. Future updates will introduce support for M-PESA Paybill and Till Number integrations, enabling broader payment options for businesses of all sizes.

Key Features

  • Instant QR Code Payments – Customers can complete transactions within seconds by scanning a merchant's QR code.
  • Real-Time Payment Confirmation – Both merchants and customers receive immediate transaction status updates.
  • Merchant Dashboard & Transaction Logs – Monitor payments, view transaction history, and track business performance.
  • Secure M-PESA Integration – Payments are processed through trusted M-PESA infrastructure with verification mechanisms.
  • Fast & Convenient Checkout – Eliminates manual number entry and speeds up customer service.
  • Digital Payment Records – Maintains accurate transaction records for accountability and business management.
  • Future-Ready Platform – Planned support for Paybill and Till Numbers.

Unnascan is committed to delivering a secure, efficient, and user-friendly digital payment ecosystem that empowers businesses to accept mobile payments with ease.

Unnapack

Unnapack is a comprehensive school management platform designed to streamline and digitize daily school operations. The system brings together student administration, fee management, academic records, staff management, transport coordination, and parent communication into a single integrated solution, helping educational institutions operate more efficiently and transparently.

By automating routine administrative tasks, Unnapack reduces paperwork, improves record accuracy, and enhances communication between schools, parents, teachers, and administrators. The platform enables schools to manage finances, monitor student progress, and access critical information in real time through a centralized dashboard.

Key Features

  • Automated Fee Collection – Supports seamless school fee payments through M-PESA Paybill integration with automatic payment reconciliation.
  • Instant SMS Notifications & Receipts – Parents receive real-time payment confirmations, receipts, and important school updates via SMS.
  • Student Information Management – Maintains comprehensive student records, including admissions, attendance, academic performance, and disciplinary history.
  • Examination & Academic Management – Facilitates exam setup, grading, report card generation, and performance analysis.
  • Staff Management – Organizes employee records, roles, responsibilities, and administrative workflows.
  • School Transport Management – Tracks transport routes, assigned vehicles, drivers, and student transportation records.
  • Parent & School Communication – Enhances engagement through timely notifications, announcements, and school updates.
  • Digital Operations Dashboard – Provides administrators with real-time insights into finances, student data, academic performance, and overall school operations.
  • Centralized Reporting & Analytics – Generates detailed reports to support informed decision-making and institutional growth.

Unnapack empowers schools to embrace digital transformation by providing a secure, reliable, and scalable platform that simplifies administration, improves efficiency, and strengthens collaboration between schools and parents.
